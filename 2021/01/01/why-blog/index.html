Why start this blog?

I am starting this blog for a several reasons:

  • I enjoy writing and need a “downhill” leisure activity (I can spend hours writing) so I get off of YouTube and Habitica.
  • It is an attempt to learn in public. My hope is that creating content that is available online will make me think more deeply about what I am communicating.
  • It will be a reference for future me and, hopefully, other people. I will be very happy if other people get some value from what I create here. Meeting people that enjoyed Robohub’s podcast was one of my favorite parts of being involved there.
  • It is a way to create value from something that I am already doing (writing).
  • This blog will grow with me as I learn more about web development.

What will I write about? Whatever interests me. I enjoyed my time with Robohub, but found the incredibly broad topic of robotics limiting. Expect to see posts on robotics and web development, but also on improv comedy, behavior change, and stoicism, as well as whatever interests me.

P.S. The RSS feed should work, if you’d like to subscribe.

P.P.S. The eclipse picture is one that I took with my Dad in 2017 in Nebraska during the total eclipse. I wrote a Python script to trigger his camera to take pictures with various exposure lengths and he combined the photos afterwards to get the image you see above.
